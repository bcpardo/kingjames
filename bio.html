James "King James" Earl was born on September 20, 1989 in the depts of South Africa. He began rapping at the young age of 13, blowing away the expectations of his peers. James decided to perform at local venues for money, and one day a talent scout happen to be attending one of these events, and was impressed by "The King". The talent scout offered James a position with a new up-and-coming rap group. At 16, he performed with the Grammy-winning group "Heavy Arms". At 18, after leaving Heavy Arms, he became a part of the hit hip-hop duo "Knees".

And at 20, he finally decided to go solo, rising up to the top with multiple chart-topping hit singles such as "Pasta", "Sweater Weather", and "Throw Up". He released his first album "Chicken Sauce" in to critical acclaim and later Pitchfork approved. The King now has 5 albums each with critical acclaim.

James now lives in Los Angeles, CA, where he is working hard on his next magnum opus album. He has a wife and no kids, with whom he met during his time with Nees.The King has since then won 5 Grammy Awards, which he calls the crown jewels, and keeps displayed in a vault-room in his house.
